Services

We at saankhya believe in students to have a dynamic approach towards science and technology. The world specially india today needs Innovative thinkers,creative imagineers and research enthusiasts. We believe that these qualities has to be instilled from an early age.Based on this we have structured our course such that it can benefit various entrance exam aspirants as well as young kids from STD VIII/IX/X .

JEE Mains and Advanced / XI and XII

As very recent aspirants/Qualifiers of IIT jee and other national level exams we believe that exams have transformed into strategy based exam.The exam on hand tests your knowledge on the other hand it also tests your speed, accuracy and and time management .Based on this we have designed the course of IIT JEE to cater all these needs and also keeping in mind the syllabus for other national and state level exams.The course gives you an entire different perspective towards IIT JEE preparation (The content,Teaching,Comprehensive study material ,Ambience….).

Olympiads/NTSE/KVPY

Enroll with us in this program to kick start your kids career .The programme covers concepts,builds aptitude,prepares you for Out of the box thinking and focuses on critical and analytical thinking to make you excel in national and international level competitions.









VIII/IX/X

Creation of creative thinkers , great innovators need to be started early when the students mind is fresh and is at a developing stage.We at saankhya wish to provide kids a platform to pursue the same.The course covers the entire curriculum of CBSE.Join our regular classroom program to make every aspect of science and technology engaging and meaningful and to excel well in board exams.
